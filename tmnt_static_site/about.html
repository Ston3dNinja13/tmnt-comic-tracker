About TMNT Comic Price Tracker

Our Mission

TMNT Comic Price Tracker is dedicated to helping collectors find the best prices for Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles comic books. Our daily price updates ensure you always have access to the most current market information.

What We Track

We currently track all 66 issues of TMNT Series 1 (1984-1993) across various marketplaces, including:

  • eBay
  • Heritage Auctions
  • ComicConnect
  • MyComicShop
  • And many more!

How It Works

Our system scans multiple comic book marketplaces daily to find the lowest prices for each issue. We record both current and previous prices, allowing you to track price trends over time.

When you find a comic at a price you like, simply click on it to be taken directly to the marketplace where it's listed.

Add Your Own Comics

Want to track comics beyond our initial TMNT Series 1 collection? Use our "Add Comic" feature to include any additional comics you're interested in. Our system will start tracking prices for those as well!

Did You Know?

The first issue of Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles was self-published by Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird in 1984 with a print run of only 3,000 copies. Today, it's one of the most valuable independent comics ever published!

Collector's Tip

When evaluating comic prices, always consider the condition. Our tracker shows the condition of each listing, helping you make informed decisions about fair market value.
